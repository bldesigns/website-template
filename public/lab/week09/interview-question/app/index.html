Buying an iPad Mini?

The iPad Mini is on of the offerings in Apple's stable of tablet computers, and - as the name suggests - differs from its older siblings in that if it is much smaller in size

Just because the iPad Mini is physicallysmaller in size doesn't mean it's smaller in computer power. The tablet, which has features such as a 5MP camera, comes in a 16 GB, 32 GB, and 64GB form. A Wi-Fi and a Wi-Fi + Cellular model are both available. As an added bonus, the iPad Mini comes in both white and black.
